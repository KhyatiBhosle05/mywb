My recipes

Red Tikka
Small pieces of boneless chicken baked using skewers on a brazier called angeethi after marinating in red Indian spices and dahi (yogurt)—essentially a boneless version of tandoori chicken.

Dumplings
Minced chicken along with vegetables wrapped in a pocked made of flour. Healthy juicy and tasty dumplings are all time comfort food.

Green Tikka
Small pieces of boneless chicken baked using skewers on a brazier called angeethi after marinating in green Indian spices and dahi (yogurt)—essentially a boneless version of tandoori chicken.

Chilli-Chicken
Chilli chicken is a sweet, spicy & slightly sour crispy appetizer made with chicken, bell peppers, garlic, chilli sauce & soya sauce. This crisp fried saucy chilli chicken recipe is hands down the best that you can make at home.

Butter Chicken
It is a dish made by marinating a chicken overnight in a yoghurt and spice mixture. The chicken is then roasted or baked. A sauce is made from butter, tomatoes, almonds and various spices, usually including the famous tandoori masala spice mix.

Kebab
Kebabs consist of cut up or ground meat, sometimes with vegetables, and various other accompaniments according to the specific recipe. Although kebabs are typically cooked on a skewer over a fire, some are baked or prepared as a stew.
